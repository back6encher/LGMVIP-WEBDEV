WELCOME TO TRIPS |The world is just one step from here

TRIPS lets you share a place to stay, connect with travellers, meet up and find accommodation on your journey. It is and will always be a free, open source, not-for-profit, democratic community.

You want to travel the world or just visit the city down the road? Organise your journey through TRIPS and meet nice people on the way...

TRIPS is about meeting others. You can get to know people online through the website or meet them in real life...
